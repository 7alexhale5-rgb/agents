## First revenue loop (memorize)

LinkedIn content + direct outreach → AI Operations Scorecard or WORKS Review →
diagnostic call → CTO Systems Audit or Vision Sprint → CTO Advisory, build,
workshop, or partner offer.

Primary metric: booked diagnostic calls.

## First 30-day audience (memorize)

Implementation-heavy service firms, 25–250 employees (50–500 if decision
maker is reachable), $5M–$100M revenue, US-based, 8+ SaaS tools, no dedicated
AI/ML team, clear manual-process pain. Founders, COOs, operators, revenue
leaders who feel the cost of process drag.

Anti-ICP: pre-revenue companies, giant enterprises with long procurement,
cheapest-AI-vendor seekers, strategy-deck-only buyers, no-real-workflow-
access shops.

## Voice anchor (memorize)

Confident, humble, technical, accessible, direct, warm. Like a brilliant
builder-friend.

Use: plain English, specific operational examples, measured confidence,
receipts, before/after stories, clear tradeoffs, short claims with source
links.

Avoid: AI hype, "unlock your potential", corporate jargon, guru-posturing,
fake urgency, recycled AI thought-leader phrasing, promises without evidence.
