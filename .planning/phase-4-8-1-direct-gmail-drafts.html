Phase 4.8.1 - Direct Gmail Drafts

Markdown source: .planning/phase-4-8-1-direct-gmail-drafts.md

Summary

Skip Zapier for the Marin draft pilot. Build one narrow Google Workspace path: marin.gmail_create_draft creates Gmail drafts through users.drafts.create and emits a redacted marin.gmail_draft.proposed event.

Operator Flow

  1. Marin produces an Alex-approved outbound draft packet.
  2. The adapter receives recipient, subject, and body inputs.
  3. It builds MIME, base64url encodes it, and calls Gmail draft creation.
  4. It returns Gmail identifiers plus hashed recipient/account metadata.
  5. PFOS receives redacted metadata only.
  6. Alex reviews and sends manually from Gmail.

Constraints

  • No Zapier or third-party broker.
  • No send, reply, forward, label, archive, delete, or inbox mutation.
  • No changes to read-only email triage.
  • No raw email body, subject, recipient, or account identifier in PFOS.
  • Credential binding stays outside the repo.

Acceptance

Unit tests cover MIME/base64url construction, Gmail draft-create invocation, absence of a send surface, Marin config exposure, and forbid_external_sends. Live Gmail draft creation now creates one harmless Gmail draft and one redacted PFOS event.
